About me

Graphic Designer | Photographer

Hi, my name is Marika and I'm a designer and photographer currently based in Philadelphia.

In 2015 I received a BFA in Graphic Design from Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA). My main interests are in publication design, branding, UI/UX design, information visualization, and photography. My mission is to continue to grow and broaden my capabilities to be able to use mix of print & multimedia for functional and delightful outcome. When I'm not working on a project, I like to excessively watch tv shows and movies, go for hiking and make flavorful tea concoctions. Feel free to send me an email with any questions you may have concerning my work, my availability for your upcoming projects or just to tell a joke.

Thanks for visiting.
